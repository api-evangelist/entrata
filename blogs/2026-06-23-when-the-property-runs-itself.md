Entrata's vision for autonomous properties shifts the operational model so routine tasks run automatically while humans focus on complex decisions. The approach relies on a unified resident record, AI agents graded on autonomy levels, nightly monitoring to catch model drift, and physical sensors to maintain property documentation, moving staff from initial triage to judgment-based work on exception cases.
